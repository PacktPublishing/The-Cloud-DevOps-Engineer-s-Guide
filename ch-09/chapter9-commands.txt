//01-terraform_prerequisite_command
terraform apply

//02-iam_roles_reference
Required IAM Roles:

1. CodePipeline Role (AWS service trusted entity – CodePipeline use case)

2. CodeBuild Role (AWS service trusted entity – CodeBuild use case)
   - Must allow:
     - CloudWatch logging
     - ECR push permissions

3. CodeDeploy Role (AWS service trusted entity – CodeDeploy use case)
   - Attach AWSCodeDeployRoleForECS policy

//03-codeguru_pipeline_integration_note
CodeGuru can be added as a pipeline action after the Source stage.
If critical issues are detected, manual approval can be required before continuing.
